MANGROVES

In Cordova, mangroves are homes for sea creatures and protect us from storm

Mangroves are awesome. They stop strong winds, keep water clean, and help baby fish grow safely

But sometimes, trash hurts them.

Taking care of mangroves means keeping Cordova beautiful, as Cordova people we should care for mangroves needed to cleaning up to keep our mangroves strong. It makes our place safe and the mangroves happy
